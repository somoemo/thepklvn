Thep tron dac 35CrMo la loai thep hop kim chua 0.32-0.40% carbon, 0.17-0.37% silic, 0.40-0.70% mangan, 0.80-1.10% chrom, 0.15-0.25% molipden va 0.15-0.25% cua cac nguyen to khac. Voi thanh phan hoa hoc da dang va can bang, thep tron dac 35CrMo co do cung cao, do ben keo lon va kha nang chong an mon tot. Loai thep nay con co kha nang chiu duoc nhiet do cao va co do deo dai tot, giup cho qua trinh gia cong va han de dang hon.

Thep tron dac 35CrMo co kich thuoc da dang, tu 6mm den 300mm, thuong duoc san xuat theo tieu chuan Trung Quoc GB/T 3077-1999. Voi dac tinh noi troi va da dang ve kich thuoc, loai thep nay thuong duoc su dung trong cac cong trinh xay dung nhu cau, tau thuyen, nha xuong, co so ha tang, v.v.
